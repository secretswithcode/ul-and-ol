BRANCHES OF CHEMISTRY


  1. Organic Chemistry
  2. Inorganic Chemistry
  3. Physical Chemistry
  4. Analytical Chemistry
Following are the compounds related to all these branches of chemistry
which we are dealing in our daily life.
Organic chemistry deals with all hydrocarbons such as,
  • Methane
  • Ethane
  • Propane
  • Methane Cloride
  • Carbon tetrachloride etc.
Inorganic chemistry deals with all minerals such as,
  • Sodium Chloride
  • Sodium carbonates
  • Bicarbonates
  • Sulphides
  • Phosphates etc.

Physical chemistrydeals with all physical phenomenas such as
  • Sublimation
  • Freezing
  • Melting
  • Boiling
  • Vapour pressure etc.

Analytical chemistrydeals with work related to equipments such as
  • Solution making
  • Molarity
  • Molality
  • Flourisence
  • Magnetism etc.
